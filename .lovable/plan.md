## Update Favicon

Replace the current `favicon.ico` in `public/` with the uploaded ENPOLARÍZATE isotype image and update `index.html` to reference it.

### Steps
1. Delete `public/favicon.ico`.
2. Copy the uploaded image (`Isotipo_Enpolarízate_sin_fondo.png`) to `public/favicon.png`.
3. Add `<link rel="icon" href="/favicon.png" type="image/png" />` to the `<head>` of `index.html`.

No other changes required.
